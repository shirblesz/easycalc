No Data Collection

TalkCalc does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data whatsoever.

What We Don't Do

Voice Input

Voice input is processed entirely by your device's built-in speech recognition. No audio is sent to our servers.

Local Storage

EasyCalc saves your theme and text size preferences locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.
